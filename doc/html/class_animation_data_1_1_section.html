Sections are animation strips to provide data for animating other objects.

Parameters
textureThe reference to the texture for the section to use.
horizontalFramesThe amount of frames left to right.
verticalFramesThe amount of frames top to bottom.
topLeftTop left corner of the animation strip.
bottomRightEither the bottom right corner or the size of the animation.
useSizeIf true, will use the size of the animations instead of the bottom right corner.
